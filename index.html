About

I have been a law enforcement professional for 15 years. I later achieved a role serving as an accident reconstructionist, which entails analyzing roadway evidence and applying principales of physics to determine the speeds and trajectory of the involved vehicles and/or beings. I have also been serving as a detective for the past 4 years. During my time in law enforcement, I have investigated more than 500 motor vehicle collisions and over 1,000 criminal investigations, made over 400 arrests, and executed 70 search warrants.

Notable Cases

  • Child Molestation Case: Involved the seizure of 30 electronic communication devices, forensic analysis of the devices, analysis of several journals and electronic documents, and an almost three hour Mirandized interrogation resulting in a full confession.

  • Serial Breaking and Entering Case: Involved preservation and a search warrant for cellular tower data to identify three suspects based on a phone call observed on surveillance. The suspects were identified based on my analysis and were linked to more than 30 other breaks across 3 states.

  • Elder Exploitation Case: An individual was accused of forging and cashing 4 checks for $25,000.00 each. Case involved search warrants for financial records and routing information. Records were analyzed as was the related networking traffic. Testified to networking principles to attribute the digital deposits to the suspect.
